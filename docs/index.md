## About the workshop

Two days of applied work with open global datasets, Python for environmental data, AI-assisted coding and core statistical methods — all directed at research questions relevant to the Danube Delta, the Black Sea coast and climate change.

Friday is dedicated to structured training; on Saturday, participants work on their own projects and deliver short pitches at the end. The workshop opens with an invited lecture by Prof. **Edward Anthony** (CEREGE, Aix-Marseille Université) on the physical processes that shape deltas.

!!! tip "Everything runs in Google Colab"
    You don't need to install anything on your laptop. Each module page has an **Open in Colab** button that opens the notebook directly in your browser, connected to your Google account. Details on the [Resources](resources.md) page.
